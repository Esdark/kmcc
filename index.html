About Us

Kerala Muslim Cultural Centre UK, popularly known as KMCC, is a non-profit organization who works in the interests of Indian expatriates based in the United Kingdom. As a well known organisationamongst Indian community in the UK, it runs several humanitarian and welfare programs for the socio-educational development of its members and also to a wider community in the UK. KMCC has always been there with relentless service force to help expatriate communities in the event of calamities and volatile circumstances abroad by providing help with embassy related help, humanitarian assistance, arranging cultural activities for the families to get connected with home culture and so on.
